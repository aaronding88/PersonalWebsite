V.0.0:

Alpha Version game released.

V.0.1:

- Player lasers, forcefields, and instant thrust has been removed.
- Player thrust now reflects enemy projectiles in addition to damaging enemies.
- U.I. Cooldown icons have been removed for a cleaner display.
- Enemy cone of fire has increased from 5 to 45 (About 60 degrees both way now).
- Enemy projectile speed reduced from 10 to 5.
- Enemy firing rate decreased. Swing timer increased from 1 to 2.
- Asteroids have been removed, to decrease confusion.
- Bug Fixes: Damaged enemies no longer respawn with below max health.
